Welcome!

I primarily draw art of my original characters and fanart of characters from various popular media. Here are some of the media I'm currently interested in creating content for:

  • Genshin Impact
  • Honkai: Star Rail
  • Pokemon
  • Project SEKAI
  • Ace Attorney
  • Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Aside from the above series, I'm a huge fan of Vocaloid and Kirby!
